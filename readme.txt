== Description ==

STREAMOTOR video plugin for WordPress is an extention of STREAMOTOR.com online video management system.  STREAMOTOR is a streaming video solution that allows you to manage your online video content by organizing, customizing and publishing your videos. In addition, STREAMOTOR provides you real-time access to valuable usage reports and analytics. 
 
Major Features in 1.0:
* Upload Video width verying degree of encoding options 
* Full suite of management tools
* Video Stats 
* Easy integration with WP tinyMCE when posting blog entry.
* Share videos to YouTube, Vimeo and Facebook.


== Installation ==

1. Unzip the contents of the Streamotor-x.x.x.zip file to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

1.  Do I need a Streamotor.com to use this plugin?

	Yes.   Streamotor, is a subscription based service providing encoding, storage and streaming of video content.

2.  Do I need a YouTube, Facebook or Vimeo accounts to use the Streamotor.com plugin?
	
	No.   YouTube, Facebook and Vimeo are added features to make it easy to share your videos to multiple social platforms.
